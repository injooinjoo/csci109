One Step Away (Chinese: 觸不可及) is a 2014 Chinese suspense romance film directed by Zhao Baogang. It was released on September 19, 2014.[1]

[edit]
  • "Ai Bu Ke Ji" (爱不可及)
    • Lyrics: Lin Xi
    • Music: Zhang Yadong
    • Singer: Faye Wong

Reception

[edit]

By September 28, it had earned ¥69.87 million at the Chinese box office.[2]
