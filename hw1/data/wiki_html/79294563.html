Troll 2 is an upcoming Norwegian monster film directed by Roar Uthaug scheduled for release in 2025. It is the sequel to Troll and it was filmed in Norway and Budapest. Troll 2 is the largest film production ever in the Nordic countries.

Production

[edit]

Troll 2 was directed by Roar Uthaug, with Espen Aukan as the screenwriter, and Espen Horn and Kristian Strand Sinkerud [no] from Motion Blur as the film producers.[4] The film was filmed in Trondheim, Maridalen, Jotunheimen, and Budapest.[5][6][7][8] According to Espen Aukan, they filmed in Budapest because of difficulties with finding filming locations in Norway.[5] Troll 2 is the largest film production ever in the Nordic countries.[8] The first promotional clip of the film was released in January 2025,[9] and the first teaser trailer was released in June 2025.[10]

Release

[edit]

Troll 2 is scheduled to release on Netflix on December 1, 2025.[10]
